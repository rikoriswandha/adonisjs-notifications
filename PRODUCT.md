# Product Context

## Product
AdonisJS Notifications — A flexible notification system for AdonisJS applications. Database-backed deliveries, multi-channel routing (mail, log, null, custom), queue support, quiet hours, and preference filtering.

## Users
- Primary: Backend developers using AdonisJS who need to observe notification health in production or staging
- Secondary: System operators checking delivery success/failure rates during incidents
- Tertiary: Engineers debugging per-notifiable delivery issues

## Product Purpose
Provide visibility into the notification pipeline. The dashboard answers: "Are my notifications sending? What's failing? Where?"

## Register
product

## Tone
Calm, precise, utilitarian. No hype. The dashboard serves engineers who are already under pressure. Information density is valued; decoration is not.

## Anti-references
- SaaS hero metrics (giant numbers with gradients)
- Sidebar-navigation app chrome (this is a single-purpose view, not a full app)
- Data-grid UIs with endless rows
- Terminal-green cyberpunk aesthetic

## Strategic Principles
- Surface signal, suppress noise
- Every element earns its place
- Prefer trends over snapshots
- Respect the user's time
